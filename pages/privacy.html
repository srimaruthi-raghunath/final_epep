Privacy & Policy

1. Introduction

ePep.in is an online platform designed to provide easy access to various newspapers' websites. Our goal is to serve as a simple portal to help students, educational departments, and other interested users conveniently access newspaper content and links to their official websites. We do not store or display the original content of the newspapers; instead, we redirect users to the official newspaper websites when a user clicks on the link.

2. What Information We Collect

We value your privacy and are committed to maintaining the confidentiality of any information you provide. Our website does not require users to create accounts or provide personal information to access the links. However, we may collect the following:

  • Cookies and Usage Data: When you visit our website, we may collect non-personally identifiable information such as your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and browsing patterns (pages you visit, time spent on the site, etc.). This data is collected for improving our website’s functionality, performance, and user experience.
  • Third-Party Analytics: We may use third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to help us understand user behavior on our website. These tools may collect certain information to provide insights on site performance, demographics, and usage trends.

3. How We Use the Information We Collect

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Website Improvement: The data we collect helps us analyze user interactions with our website so that we can improve the functionality, design, and user experience of the platform.
  • Enhancing User Experience: We may use the data to personalize your experience and tailor the content shown on our website according to your preferences.
  • Legal and Compliance: We may also use the information to comply with legal obligations or enforce our terms of service.

4. No Original Content Hosting

We want to emphasize that our platform does not host or store any original content from the newspapers we link to. Our service simply provides easy access to users by redirecting them to the official newspaper portals. All content available through our links is the sole property of the respective newspapers. By clicking on the newspaper links, users are directed to the official websites where they can view the content directly from the original source.

Our platform does not alter, modify, or store any newspaper articles, images, or content. We do not control the content on external websites, and we are not responsible for any changes made to those websites.

5. Third-Party Websites

Our website contains links to third-party websites, including those of newspapers, educational portals, and other relevant sites. These third-party websites are operated by independent entities, and we have no control over the content, privacy policies, or practices of those sites. We strongly encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites before providing any personal information.

By providing links to external websites, we do not endorse or guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or reliability of the content on those sites.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience while visiting our website. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you access our website. They help us recognize your browser, remember your preferences, and gather website usage data for analytics purposes.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but please note that doing so may limit some features or functionality of our website.

We may also use third-party tracking technologies (such as Google Analytics) to analyze user behavior on our website, improve the site's performance, and personalize your experience. These services may collect and use data about your interactions with our website.

7. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and have implemented measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, please note that no data transmission or storage method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of any information you share with us. By using our website, you acknowledge that you understand the inherent risks of online data transmission.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so that we can take the necessary steps to remove such data from our systems.

9. Your Rights and Choices

As a user of our website, you have the following rights concerning your personal information:

  • Access: You have the right to request access to any personal data we may have collected about you.
  • Correction: If you believe any personal data we hold about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request correction.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal information at any time. However, please note that we may retain some information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.
  • Opt-Out of Cookies: You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings, including blocking or deleting cookies.

We reserve the right to update and modify this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
